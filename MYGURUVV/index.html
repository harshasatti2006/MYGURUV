ABOUT MYGURU

Vote. Appreciate. Celebrate your Gurus.

myGuruV is a student-driven platform that empowers learners to recognize and appreciate exceptional educators. With a simple and secure voting system, we make it easy for students to celebrate the mentors who have made a meaningful impact on their academic journey.

  • To be the most trusted platform for teacher recognition in education.
  • To build a culture of gratitude and inspiration in every institution.
  • To encourage excellence in education through positive feedback.
  • To amplify the voices of students in recognizing great teaching.
